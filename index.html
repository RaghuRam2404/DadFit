Building Sustainable Fitness

I joined a gym in late 2019 and learned the fundamentals. When COVID hit, I turned to YouTube, experimented, and found what worked for my body. By the end of 2024, I completed my Personal Training and Nutrition Certificate courses from NASM, becoming a Certified Personal Trainer. I work out 3-4 days per week, proving that sustainable fitness fits into real life.
