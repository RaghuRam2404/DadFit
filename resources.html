Optimal Workout Sequence

Dynamic Warm-Up

5-10 minutes: movement prep, light cardio, elevate heart rate, increase muscle temperature

Dynamic Stretches for neck and upper/lower body

Core Activation & Stabilisation work

McGill Big 3, planks—engage stabilisers

Rotator cuff exercises, before doing upper body workouts

Main Workout

Compound lifts first, then isolation—train in order of movement complexity

Cardio Finisher

10 minutes HIIT or steady-state—cardiovascular conditioning without compromising strength

Static Stretching

Cool-down phase—improve flexibility whilst muscles are warm, aid recovery

Dynamic Stretches (Warm-ups)

Rotator Cuff and Hip Flexor Focus

Exercises

Core - McGill Big 3

  • McGill Curl-Up - Targets rectus abdominis, obliques for spinal stability
  • Plank
  • Shoulder Tap

Chest

  • Flat Barbell Bench Press - Overall chest development, compound strength builder
  • Incline Dumbbell Press - Upper chest emphasis (clavicular head)
  • Decline Bench Press - Lower chest focus (costal head)
  • Chest Fly - Pectoral isolation through horizontal adduction

Back

Training principle: vertical movements build back width, horizontal movements develop back thickness

  • Pull-Ups - Dominant: lats, biceps brachii, brachialis, teres major, rhomboids
  • V-Grip Lat Pulldown - Lat emphasis with controlled resistance
  • Wide Grip Row - Middle/lower traps, rhomboids, rear delts for width and thickness
  • Narrow Grip Row - Lower lat emphasis, increased biceps involvement
  • Face Pull - Posterior deltoids, trapezius, rhomboids for shoulder health
  • Deadlift - Complete posterior chain: back, glutes, hamstrings, core, forearms

Shoulders

  • Overhead DB Press - Complete shoulder development, triceps engagement, core stability
  • Lateral Raise
  • Cable Lateral Raise - Lateral deltoid isolation, shoulder width development
  • Front Raise - Anterior deltoid targeting, shoulder flexion emphasis
  • Rear Delt Fly
  • Shrugs - trapezius development, scapular elevation strength

Biceps

  • Incline Dumbbell Curl - Maximises biceps stretch, emphasises long head
  • Hammer Curl - Emphasises brachioradialis and forearm muscles
  • Cross-Body Hammer Curl - Emphasises brachialis, builds biceps thickness
  • Concentration Curl - Pure biceps isolation, eliminates momentum

Triceps

  • DB Skull Crushers - Full muscle stretch of long and lateral head
  • Cable Triceps Kickback - Contraction of long & lateral head
  • Reverse Grip Cable Pushdown - Targets medial head (do at last)

Forearms

  • Wrist Curls - Forearm flexors, palm-up position, wrist flexion movement
  • Reverse Wrist Curls - Forearm extensors, palm-down position
  • Grip Strength Training - Farmer's carries, dead hangs, gripper work

Legs

  • Squats - Quads, glutes, hamstrings, core (the king of lower body exercises)
  • Hip Thrust - Glute and hamstring emphasis, superior glute activation
  • Romanian Deadlift - Hamstrings, glutes, lower back, hip hinge pattern mastery
  • Lunges - Quads, glutes, hamstrings, unilateral strength and stability
  • Calf Raises - Seated: soleus emphasis | Standing: gastrocnemius focus

Abs

  • Crunches variation & leg raise variation
